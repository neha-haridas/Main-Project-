Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does the mess follow a food chart

    Yes, it follows the food chart for Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks, and Dinner

  • Are there A/C and Non A/c rooms for hostellers?

  • What are the visiting hrs. in hostel for parents?

    4:00 pm to 6:00pm

  • What about Medical faclilites in hostel?

    We have 24 hrs. first Aid Medical facility and our nursing staff daily visit to the hostel.

  • Whether mobile is allowed?

    No, mobile is not allowed for 24 hours in the hostel. We have a telephone facility and intercom facility in each room in hostel for students. Mobiles are issued for 1:30 hours in the evening (Provided the parent of the hosteller gives permission for that).

  • What is the Hostel fee?

    There are 2 options : Option-1 (One time payment) : Rs. 75,000/- per annum + Rs. 10,000 for Security deposit which is refundable.
    Option-2 (Payment in installment including security deposit) : Rs. 47,000/- at the time of admission and Rs. 46,000/- payable on or before 31st October of the academic year
